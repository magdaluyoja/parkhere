Project Name: ParkHare
Description: A sample project about an automatic parking system that displays sample available parking areas and spaces. Users need to sigup and login to use the app. When logged in, they can choose their desired parking area and space then the app automatically records the time and amount to be paid when unparking. This has a sample online payment only.

Technology Used: Meteor 1.5
